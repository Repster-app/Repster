What makes Reppo different

Smart Suggestions & Adaptive Fatigue Learning

Reppo doesn't just track your workouts — it understands your fatigue and prescribes weights that match your readiness.

Your weight, calculated for you

Based on your recent performance, current fatigue level, and rest between sets, Reppo recommends the right weight for every set. It gets more accurate over time as it learns your personal fatigue patterns.

Fatigue-Aware

Tracks how fatigue accumulates through your session and accounts for rest-based recovery between sets.

Learns Over Time

Compares predictions to your actual performance and adjusts its fatigue model per exercise — globally and individually.

Fully Configurable

Set your target reps, target RIR, recency window, and more. You stay in control of the prescription logic.

Features

Everything you need to train

A complete workout tracker built for lifters who care about progress.

🏋

Focused Workout Mode

A distraction-free interface for logging sets. Switch between exercises with tabs, track weight and reps, and let the rest timer handle pacing.

🏆

Automatic PR Detection

Personal records are detected the moment you complete a set. Badges show current, previous, and matched PRs across your entire history.

📚

Exercise Library

Browse and filter by muscle group, create custom exercises, and choose from multiple tracking types — weight+reps, duration, distance, and more.

📋

Templates

Save any workout as a template, create them from scratch, or use the AI template generator with ChatGPT to build new routines.

📈

Charts & Analytics

Visualize your progress with muscle group breakdowns, workout frequency trends, and per-exercise performance charts over any time period.

📅

Calendar

A monthly calendar view with color-coded workout indicators. Tap any day to see workout details, muscle groups trained, and total volume.

⚖

Body Weight Tracking

Log your bodyweight over time. Entries are used in effective weight calculations for bodyweight-factor exercises.

💾

Import & Export

Import workout history from CSV (FitNotes format), create full backups, and restore your data anytime.

🏠

Customizable Dashboard

Your home screen shows weekly activity, recent workouts, monthly stats, and recent PRs — and you can toggle each section on or off.
