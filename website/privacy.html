Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Overview

Reppo is designed with your privacy in mind. Your workout data is stored entirely on your device and is never uploaded to our servers. We do not track your activity, collect analytics, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Storage

All workout data — including exercises, sets, personal records, templates, fatigue learning data, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

  • Workout history — stored on-device only
  • Exercise library — stored on-device only
  • Personal records — stored on-device only
  • Templates — stored on-device only
  • Body weight entries — stored on-device only
  • Fatigue learning data — stored on-device only
  • App settings and preferences — stored on-device only

Third-Party Services

Reppo uses one third-party service:

  • RevenueCat — used solely for managing subscriptions and in-app purchases. RevenueCat processes your purchase through Apple's App Store and receives only the information necessary to verify your subscription status. For details on how RevenueCat handles data, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Reppo does not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, or advertising frameworks. There is no tracking of any kind.

AI Template Feature

Reppo includes an optional AI template generator that helps you create workout templates using ChatGPT. This feature works by generating a prompt that you manually copy and paste into ChatGPT yourself. Reppo does not connect to OpenAI or any AI service directly — you control what is shared and when.

Data Export & Backup

You can export your workout data as a backup file or CSV at any time through the Settings screen. Exported files are saved to a location of your choosing on your device. Reppo does not upload backup files to any server.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time using the "Reset App Data" option in Settings. This permanently removes all workouts, exercises, personal records, templates, and learning data from your device. Since no data is stored on external servers, there is nothing else to delete.

Uninstalling the app also removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Reppo does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or collect any identifying information.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how Reppo handles your data, please contact us at support@reppo.app.
