Privacy Policy

Repster is designed so your workout data stays on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

Overview

Repster stores workout data locally on your device. We do not operate workout-data servers, user accounts, advertising systems, or analytics tracking for your training activity.

Repster does not sell your personal information.

Data Stored On Device

The app stores training data locally using Apple's app storage technologies. Local data can include:

  • Workout history, exercises, sets, notes, and workout titles.
  • Personal records, templates, and exercise library changes.
  • Bodyweight entries, fatigue learning data, and recommendation settings.
  • App settings such as units, formulas, rest timers, import settings, and subscription access state.

This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly export, share, back up, or otherwise move it outside the app.

Subscriptions and Purchases

Repster uses RevenueCat to manage subscription status and in-app purchase entitlement checks. Purchases are processed by Apple's App Store. RevenueCat receives purchase and device/app information needed to validate subscription access.

RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy. RevenueCat's terms are available at revenuecat.com/terms.

Repster does not receive your full payment card details. Payment processing, cancellation, renewal, and refund handling are managed through Apple and your Apple ID.

No Analytics, Ads, or Tracking

Repster does not use third-party advertising frameworks. Repster does not track your workout activity for advertising or analytics purposes.

If the app uses system-level diagnostics supplied by Apple, those diagnostics are controlled by your device settings and Apple's policies.

AI Template Feature

Repster may include an optional AI template helper. This feature prepares prompt text for you to copy into ChatGPT or another AI tool manually. Repster does not directly connect to OpenAI or another AI provider for that workflow.

You control what you copy, paste, submit, or import. Do not include information in an AI prompt that you do not want to share with the AI service you choose to use.

Export, Backup, and Sharing

You can export workout data, CSV files, templates, or backups from the app. Exported files are saved or shared only where you choose, such as Files, iCloud Drive, email, messaging apps, or other storage locations available on your device.

Once you export or share data outside Repster, that destination's privacy practices apply.

Data Deletion

You can delete local app data using reset options in Repster where available. You can also remove local app data by uninstalling the app from your device.

Because Repster does not run workout-data servers, there is no separate Repster server copy of your workout history to delete. Subscription records held by Apple or RevenueCat are handled under their own policies.

Children's Privacy

Repster is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the last-updated date at the top of this page will be updated. Material changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@reppo.app.

See also the Repster Terms of Use.
