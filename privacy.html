Privacy Policy

Repster is designed so your workout data stays on your device unless you choose to export it.

Overview

Repster stores workout data locally on your device. We do not run user accounts, workout-data servers, advertising systems, or analytics tracking for your training activity.

Data Storage

Workout history, exercises, sets, personal records, templates, bodyweight entries, fatigue learning data, and app settings are stored on-device using Apple's app storage technologies.

This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly export or share it.

Third-Party Services

Repster may use RevenueCat for subscription and in-app purchase management. Purchase processing is handled through Apple's App Store, and RevenueCat receives only the information needed to verify subscription status.

RevenueCat's policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

AI Template Feature

If an AI template helper is used, Repster prepares prompt content for you to copy into ChatGPT manually. Repster does not directly connect to OpenAI or another AI service for that workflow.

Export, Backup, and Deletion

You can export workout data or backups from Settings and save them where you choose. Repster does not upload backup files to a server.

You can delete app data by using reset options in the app or by uninstalling the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

Repster does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation.

Changes and Contact

If this policy changes, the last-updated date on this page will change. For privacy questions, contact support@reppo.app.
