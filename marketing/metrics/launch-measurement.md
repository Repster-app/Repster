# Launch Measurement Plan

## Primary Funnel

Track these in App Store Connect, RevenueCat, and any in-app analytics stack that is already approved for the app:

1. Product page views
2. Product page conversion rate
3. Installs
4. Onboarding completion
5. First workout started
6. First workout completed
7. Paywall views
8. Trial, subscription, or lifetime purchase conversion
9. Day 1 retention
10. Day 7 retention

## Creative Tracking

Use a simple campaign naming scheme:

- `launch_appstore_default`
- `launch_static_log_sets`
- `launch_static_templates`
- `launch_static_prs`
- `launch_video_history_01`
- `launch_video_templates_04`
- `launch_video_charts_10`

## First Iteration Rules

After 300-500 product page views:

- If product page conversion is weak, test screenshot 1 against templates or PRs as the lead image.
- If installs are fine but first-workout completion is weak, inspect onboarding and demo-start friction.
- If first-workout completion is fine but paywall conversion is weak, test paywall copy and timing.
- If retention is weak, use lifecycle messaging and in-app prompts around the next planned workout.

## App Store Optimization

Keep the first keyword set narrow:

`workout,logger,strength,lifting,gym,weightlifting,sets,reps,tracker,training,fitness,bodybuilding`

After the first release, review:

- Search terms producing impressions
- Product page conversion by source
- Screenshot performance through product page optimization
- Review language for user phrasing to reuse in metadata
