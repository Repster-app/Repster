Repster Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

Overview

Repster is a workout logging app for iOS. Workout history, exercises, templates, bodyweight entries, and settings are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

Workout Data

Repster stores your workout data on device so the app can show history, charts, records, templates, and active workout context. Repster does not send exercise names, set weights, reps, notes, CSV contents, bodyweight values, or raw workout logs to product analytics.

Anonymous Product Analytics

Repster uses PostHog EU cloud for anonymous product analytics. Analytics help us understand aggregate app usage, such as screens viewed, workouts started, workouts completed or discarded, whether a workout lasted under 30 minutes or 30 minutes or more, paywall actions, import completion, and backup export.

Analytics are enabled by default and can be turned off in the app at Settings -> Data & Backups -> Share Anonymous Analytics.

Purchases

Repster uses RevenueCat to manage App Store purchases and entitlements. RevenueCat may process purchase history and anonymous app user identifiers to validate purchases, unlock paid features, prevent fraud, and provide purchase analytics.

What Repster Does Not Do

Repster does not use analytics for advertising, does not sell data, does not use IDFA, and does not enable PostHog session replay, autocapture, heatmaps, surveys, or person profiles.

Support

If you contact Repster for support, we use the information you provide only to respond to your request.
