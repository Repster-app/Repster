# Social Launch Kit

## Asset Outputs

- Static feed posts: `marketing/generated/social/static/`
- Video cover frames: `marketing/generated/social/video-covers/`
- Recommended vertical video format: `1080 x 1920`, 9:16, 15-25 seconds.

## Short-Form Video Scripts

Each hook has three variants. Use real screen recordings under the cover frame and keep captions readable without sound.

1. Show active workout history.
   Caption: `Previous sets, PRs, and rest timing stay in the workout.`
   CTA: `Try Repster for your next lift.`

2. Show history tab, then return to set logging.
   Caption: `Know what you lifted last time before the next set.`
   CTA: `Download Repster.`

3. Show home PRs, then active workout history.
   Caption: `Stop rebuilding context between every session.`
   CTA: `Track smarter without extra setup.`

## Channel Notes

- TikTok, Reels, Shorts: lead with the hook in the first frame, then show the UI action immediately.
- X: post the static image with one sentence and the App Store link.
- Reddit: avoid sales language. Use a transparent launch post in relevant self-promotion or indie app threads only.
- Product Hunt or indie communities: emphasize local-first tracking, fast logging, and useful history during workouts.
