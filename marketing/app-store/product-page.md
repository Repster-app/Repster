## Preview Video Storyboard

Length: 20-30 seconds.

Format: App Store preview, device-captured UI footage, muted-first design.

Timeline:

- `0-3s`: Open Repster and start a workout from a template. On-screen copy: `Start fast from a saved routine`.
- `3-9s`: Log weight, reps, RIR, and rest. On-screen copy: `Log the set without slowing down`.
- `9-15s`: Show previous sets, Smart Suggestions, rest timer, and Live Activity if available. On-screen copy: `Your history stays in the workout`.
- `15-22s`: Show PRs, exercise history, and charts. On-screen copy: `Records and progress build automatically`.
- `22-30s`: Close on logo and CTA. On-screen copy: `Repster - Workout Tracking for Lifters`.

The first three seconds must communicate the value without audio.

## Capture Checklist

- Use a clean demo database with realistic lifting data.
- Use dark mode to match existing app visuals.
- Capture 6.9-inch iPhone App Store sizes when possible.
- Keep time, battery, and network indicators clean and consistent.
- Avoid medical, injury, diagnosis, or coaching claims.
- Confirm copy remains readable at App Store search-result scale.
