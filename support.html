Support

Answers to common Repster questions and a direct support contact when you need more help.

How do I start a workout?

Use the start workout card on the Home screen. You can start empty, copy a previous workout, or launch a saved template.

How are personal records detected?

Repster evaluates completed sets against your exercise history and flags records when a set represents a meaningful new best for that exercise and rep range.

Can I import data from another app?

Yes. Repster supports CSV import flows for moving existing workout history into the app. Imported data is previewed and validated before it is saved.

How do I back up my data?

Use Settings to export a backup file. Save that file somewhere safe. You can restore from a backup later from the same area of Settings.

Is my workout data stored in the cloud?

No. Workout history, exercises, templates, records, and settings are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly export them.

How does the subscription work?

The free tier lets you try the app. Full access unlocks unlimited workout logging and is managed through Apple's App Store subscriptions.

Can I track unilateral exercises?

Yes. Exercises can support left/right tracking so single-arm or single-leg work can be logged accurately.

Still need help?

Email support and include what you were trying to do, what happened, and which iPhone/iOS version you are using.
